Pig Game

Pig is a classic dice game that offers a blend of luck and strategic decision-making. The game is played with a single die and the goal is to be the first player to reach 100 points. On each turn, players roll the die, accumulating points by adding the numbers rolled. However, if a player rolls a "1," they lose all the points accumulated during that turn and their turn ends. The game presents an interesting dilemma: do players take a risk and continue rolling to amass more points or do they play it safe and stop to secure their current score? This risk-reward dynamic adds excitement and tension to each round, making Pig an engaging and entertaining game for all ages.

Sudoko Solver

Sudoku is a popular logic-based puzzle game played on a 9x9 grid. The objective is to fill in the empty cells with digits from 1 to 9, ensuring that each row, column, and 3x3 sub-grid contains all the numbers exactly once. The game provides a partially filled grid as a starting point, and the player must use deduction and logical reasoning to determine the correct placement of numbers. With its countless possible combinations and varying levels of difficulty, Sudoku offers a challenging and stimulating experience that tests one's problem-solving skills and patience, making it an addictive and enjoyable game for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages.

Guessing Game

The Guessing Game is a classic interactive game where a player tries to guess a secret number chosen by the computer. The player starts by making an initial guess, and the computer provides feedback on whether the guess is too high or too low. Based on this feedback, the player adjusts their subsequent guesses, narrowing down the possibilities with each attempt. The game continues until the player successfully guesses the secret number. The Guessing Game is an engaging and entertaining way to test one's intuition and logical thinking skills while enjoying the challenge of deducing the hidden number within a limited number of attempts.
